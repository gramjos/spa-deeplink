# Desert Biome

Deserts are arid regions receiving less than 25cm of rainfall annually.

## Types

- **Hot deserts** - Sahara, Mojave
- **Cold deserts** - Gobi, Antarctic

## Adaptations

Plants and animals have unique adaptations:
- Cacti store water in stems
- Camels have humps for fat storage
- Nocturnal behavior to avoid heat
