The Arctic

The northernmost region of Earth, home to the Arctic Ocean.

Wildlife

  • Polar Bears - Apex predators of the ice
  • Arctic Foxes - Masters of camouflage
  • Walruses - Tusked giants of the sea
  • Snowy Owls - Silent hunters

Climate Crisis

The Arctic is warming twice as fast as the global average, threatening:

  • Sea ice extent
  • Polar bear habitats
  • Indigenous communities
